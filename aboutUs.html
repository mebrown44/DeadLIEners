About Us

The Application

Our project aims to improve user productivity by taking their scheduled due dates and setting them before they are actually due to give users a sense of urgency to decrease procrastination!

As we were brainstorming as a group we envisioned problems that people face across many demographics. One of the main problems that came to mind was ultimately productivity and distractedness. We as a group all struggled with that individually and know other college students, adults, and high schoolers have similar issues. Our solution was to create a software that would create fake deadlines for the user to get them over the most difficult step of completing a task, which is of course starting early.

Why It Matters

Procrastination is a problem for many across different demographics and people end up finding creative ways to avoid what they need to do. This ultimately causes so much more stress than necessary and affects more than just the person involved. Our application aims to combat that and helps users make their deadlines on time with one less layer of worry. Knowing there is time but being encouraged to finish your tasks regardless is an innovative approach to productivity software and can benefit many.
